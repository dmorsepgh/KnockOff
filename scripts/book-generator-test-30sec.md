Here's something most people don't realize. You probably have a book inside you right now. Maybe it's your business expertise. Maybe it's a guide you wish existed when you started. Maybe it's lessons you've learned that could help thousands of people.

But here's the problem. When you sit down to write it, nothing happens. Blank page syndrome. You stare at the cursor. You type a paragraph, delete it, type it again. Writing is hard. It's slow. It's painful.
