Go to showrunner.dmpgh.com slash jobs. You'll see available jobs. Each one is either a HeyGen job with two scripts or a Pictory job with three scripts. The process is the same for both.

Enter your name and click the green Claim button. A one-hour timer starts. That's plenty of time.

Now you'll see the scripts with copy buttons. Click Copy on the first script. Open the rendering tool in a new tab, paste the script, click render, and wait a few minutes. Download the MP4 when it's done. Go back and repeat for each script in the job.

Once all your renders are downloaded, go back to the job board. Drag your MP4 files into the upload area. When all files are uploaded, the job is marked complete and you've earned a dollar.

That's it. Copy, paste, render, upload. Fifteen minutes, a dollar earned.

A couple tips. Don't close the job board tab while your timer is running. If a render fails, just try again. And if you can't finish, click Release Job so someone else can take it.
