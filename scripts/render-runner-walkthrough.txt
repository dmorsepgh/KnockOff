Welcome to Render Monkey. Here's exactly how to become a Render Runner and start earning money from home.

Step one. Go to showrunner.dmpgh.com slash jobs. You'll see a registration form at the top. Enter your name, phone number, and how you want to get paid — Venmo, Cash App, PayPal, or Apple Pay. Hit Register.

Step two. You need to complete two practice renders before you can claim real jobs. Click Practice HeyGen. You'll see a script with full instructions. Click Copy Script, then click Open HeyGen. Paste the script in, let HeyGen generate the video, and download the MP4 when it's done. Come back and upload it to prove you did it.

Step three. Do the same thing for Pictory. Click Practice Pictory, copy the script, open Pictory, paste it in, render, download, and upload. Once both practices are uploaded, you're qualified. Real jobs unlock.

Step four. When jobs are posted, you'll get a text message. Go to the job board and claim one before someone else does. Higher-rated runners get notified first and have a fifteen-minute head start.

Step five. Open the job, copy the script, click Open HeyGen or Open Pictory, paste it in, render it, download the MP4, and upload it back to the job board. You have one hour from when you claim it.

Step six. After you upload, Doug reviews your render. If it's good, the job is approved and you get paid through whatever method you chose at registration.

That's it. Register, practice, qualify, claim, render, upload, get paid. Welcome to the team.
