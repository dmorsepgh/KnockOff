# KnockOff Explainer Script

Every creator knows this pain. You want to make professional talking-head videos. Maybe for your business, your course, your YouTube channel. So you look at tools like HeyGen or Synthesia.

And then you see the pricing. Thirty dollars a month. Fifty dollars a month. Some plans hit hundreds. And that's with limits. Minutes per month. Watermarks on free tiers. Your content, processed on someone else's servers.

Here's what really gets me. The AI models that power these tools? Most of them are open source. Free. Available to anyone. But these companies package them up, slap a subscription on top, and charge you monthly for what your own computer could do.

You're not paying for the technology. You're paying for convenience. And that convenience comes with a recurring bill that never stops.

What if you could run the whole thing locally? On your own machine. No subscriptions. No API costs. No upload limits. Just your hardware, doing the work.

That's KnockOff.

[OVERLAY: phone-mockups.png | 6s]

It's a local AI video generation pipeline. You give it a script and an avatar video. It speaks the script. It syncs the lips. It outputs a professional talking-head video. All without touching the cloud.

The name says it all. We're knocking off the expensive subscription model. Taking back control of our own content creation.

The pipeline is simple but powerful.

First, text-to-speech. We use Piper TTS. It runs locally, it's fast, and the voices sound natural. No sending your scripts to external APIs.

[BROLL: remote-hands.mp4 | 5s]

Second, lip synchronization. Wav2Lip takes your avatar video and the generated audio, then creates perfect lip sync. The mouth moves naturally with every word.

Third, everything stays on your machine. Your scripts. Your avatar footage. Your output videos. Nothing leaves your computer unless you want it to.

The workflow is one command. Point it at your script, point it at your avatar video, and let it run. Minutes later, you have a finished video.

This is just the beginning.

We're building toward a complete local studio. Multiple avatars. Voice cloning with your own voice samples. B-roll integration for dynamic cuts. Batch processing for course creators who need dozens of videos.

Imagine generating an entire video course overnight. Your avatar. Your voice. Your content. Zero monthly fees.

The tools exist. The models are available. We just need to connect them in ways that put creators first.

You can start using KnockOff today.

Clone the repo. Install the dependencies. Record a short avatar video of yourself or use stock footage. Write your script. Run the command.
