It only gets easier from here.

That is what everyone says about A.I. The tools are improving every month. Six months from now, whatever you are trying to do will be faster, cheaper, easier. So why struggle through the hard version when the easy version is around the corner?

Airtight logic. Completely wrong.

Here is what it misses. Yes, the tools get easier. But they get easier for everyone. You are not building an advantage by waiting. You are treading water while the tide comes in.

The person who starts today, fumbles around, ships something bad, then ships something less bad, that person is building something A.I. cannot give you. Judgment. Taste. The ability to tell a good output from a mediocre one. When the tools get easier, they use those easier tools better than someone who waited.

There is also a window closing you cannot see. Right now, a regular person using A.I. to build something is remarkable. In eighteen months, it is table stakes.

And the real trap, this logic has no finish line. It was a good reason to wait in 2023. Still a good reason today. There will always be a reason to wait six more months.

The logic is airtight. The conclusion is wrong.

Stop waiting for easier. Start doing hard.
