                                            # Work From Home Opportunity — No Experience Needed

I'm looking for two types of people for a small project I built. You can do either one, or both.

## Render Runners — $1 per job

I produce AI-generated news shows for online communities. The scripts are written by AI. The editing is automated. The one thing I need help with is the rendering — copying a script, pasting it into a video tool, clicking render, and uploading the finished file.

That's the whole job. Copy, paste, click, upload. About 15-20 minutes per job. $1 each.

**What you need:**
- A computer with a browser and internet
- That's it

**What you don't need:**
- Experience
- Special software
- To leave your house
- To be on camera

There's a practice mode on the site so you can try it before committing to anything. If you can copy and paste, you can do this.

I built this specifically with people in mind who want to earn extra income from home but can't do traditional work. It's not a lot of money per job, but it's honest, simple work you can do on your own schedule.

## Sellers — $2.50 per video sold

I also need people who like talking to others and can spread the word about what we do. You don't need to be a salesperson. You just need to share a link and have a conversation.

Your first sale earns you $2.50 — that's half the sale price. And every time that same client buys again, you earn 11.3% ($0.57) automatically. Recurring. Forever. No caps, no limits.

**What you'd do:**
- Share the invite link in communities, groups, social media
- Talk to people who run online communities about getting their own news show
- When they sign up, you get paid

**What you don't need:**
- Sales experience
- A website
- To handle any production work

## The Breakdown

**First video sold** = $5 total
- $2.50 goes to the seller (50%)
- $1.00 goes to the Render Runner
- $1.50 covers production

**Every recurring sale** (same client buys again)
- $0.57 goes to the original seller (11.3% — forever)
- $1.00 goes to the Render Runner
- $3.43 covers production
